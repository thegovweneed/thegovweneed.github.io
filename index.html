#1: Bruce Schneier on Security

Bruce Schneier is an American cryptographer, computer security professional, privacy specialist and writer. Schneier is a fellow at the Berkman Center for Internet & Society at Harvard University and a lecturer at the Harvard Kennedy School of Government. He is the author of over one dozen books, including his latest, Click Here to Kill Everybody. (Photo: Erik Nilsson)
